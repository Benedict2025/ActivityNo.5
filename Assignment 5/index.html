The POKEMON Company

The Pokémon Company is a Japanese company responsible for the brand management, production, publishing, marketing, and licensing of the Pokémon franchise. It was founded in 1998 as a joint venture between Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures, the three companies that originally held the copyright and trademark of Pokémon. The company's headquarters is located in Tokyo, Japan.
